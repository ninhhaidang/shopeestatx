## Detailed Description (paste into Developer Dashboard)

---

ShopeeStatX solves what Shopee doesn't offer: a clear view of how much you've spent, which shops you buy from most, and how your spending changes over time.

**Key Features:**

📊 Spending Overview
• Total orders and total spending (including vouchers/discounts)
• Average order value
• Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons

📈 Interactive Charts
• Bar chart: monthly or daily spending, order count, or product count
• Pie chart: top shops (3/5/10/15 shops)
• Click a month bar to drill down into daily view
• Click a day to filter the data table

🔍 Smart Filters
• Search by product name, shop name, or order ID
• Filter by year, month, and order status (7 types)
• Filter chips show active filters — remove individually or all at once

📋 Detailed Data Table
• Full order information with status badges
• Sort by any column
• Expand rows to see individual product details
• Pagination: 20 / 50 / 100 / all

📥 Excel Export
• Export filtered data as .xlsx
• Vietnamese headers, ready for further analysis

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
• / to open search
• Escape to clear search
• R to refresh data

**How It Works:**

The extension uses your existing Shopee browser session — no separate account, no stored passwords. Simply log in to Shopee as usual, click "Start Analysis", and wait a few seconds while your order history loads.

**Security & Privacy:**

• All data stays entirely on your device
• No server, no data transmitted externally
• No personal information collected
• No account required

**Notes:**
• Only works on shopee.vn
• Requires a logged-in Shopee account
• Not officially affiliated with Shopee
